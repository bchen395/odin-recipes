Turkey Chili

Description

This is a delicious recipe by NYT Cooking for an easy weeknight dinner. I have made this recipe so many times...

Would you try this? Let me know in my email: ilovechili@gmail.com

Ingredients

  • Ground Turkey
  • Beans
  • Bell Pepper
  • Jalapeno

Steps

  1. Chop all of your veggies
  2. Brown the turkey
  3. Add your tomatoes and beans
  4. Cook for 30 min
