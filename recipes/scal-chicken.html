Sheet Pan Scallion Chicken

Description

This is a delicious recipe by NYT Cooking for an easy weeknight dinner. I have never tried this recipe...

Would you try this? Let me know in my email: ilovechicken@gmail.com

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Scallion
  • Pan

Steps

  1. Coat chicken with marinade
  2. Put chicken on sheet pan
  3. Scatter scallions over the chicken
  4. Bake in oven for 30 min at 400 degrees
