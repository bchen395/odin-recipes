Peanut Butter Chocolate Cookies

Description

This is a delicious recipe by NYT Cooking for an easy vegan dessert. I have indeed tried this recipe! It tastes delicious

Would you try this? Let me know in my email: ilovecookies@gmail.com

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Light Brown Sugar
  • Banana

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients
  2. Scoop 1.5 oz scoops on a cookie sheet
  3. Bake for 10-12 minutes
  4. Let cookies cool completely on a wire rack
